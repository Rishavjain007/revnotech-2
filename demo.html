About Revnotech Research

Bridging the Gap Between Data and Decisions

Headquartered in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh, Revnotech Research is a dedicated market research and data analytics firm. We believe that data is not just numbers—it's the narrative of your market.

With a client-centric approach, we dive deep into consumer behavior, market trends, and industry shifts to deliver insights that are actionable, accurate, and transparent. Whether you are in Pharma, FMCG, or Media, our tailored solutions help you stay ahead of the curve.

Our Expertise

Comprehensive Research Solutions

We offer specialized services designed to uncover opportunities and mitigate risks.

Healthcare & Pharma Research

In-depth analysis for healthcare providers and pharmaceutical companies. We cover market sizing, patient journey mapping, and KOL interviews to navigate the complex medical landscape.

Brand & Consumer Research

Understanding the 'Why' behind the 'Buy'. We conduct brand equity studies, concept testing, and usage & attitude studies to position your brand effectively in the consumer's mind.

Media & Opinion Research

gauging public sentiment and media consumption habits. Our opinion polls and media tracking services help in crafting messages that resonate with your target audience.

Loyalty & Customer Experience

Turn customers into advocates. We measure NPS (Net Promoter Score), CSAT (Customer Satisfaction), and identify friction points in the customer journey to enhance loyalty.

Survey-based Data Collection

Robust fieldwork capabilities. We utilize CATI, CAWI, and Face-to-Face methodologies to gather high-quality data from diverse demographics across India.
